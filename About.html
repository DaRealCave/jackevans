I’m a visual artist studying Fine Art at Falmouth University. As of typing I’m in first year and my practice revolves around subjects of politics, existentialism and contrasting mediums of analog media and digital whilst also using the traditional forms of painting and sculpture to create and visually express my ideas. I do commissions for work such as album art, visual projections and photography.
